Daffodil International University

Being established in 2002, Daffodil International University has become a prominent
private university in Bangladesh. Our 150 Acre campus is full of life and positive energy,
fostering education and innovation for more than 20000 students. Here, students are encouraged
to ask questions and think, debate and collaborate with professors to take ownership of their learning.
